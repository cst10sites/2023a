Fried Chicken

Preparation

In order to cook fried chicken, you will need:

  • 4 cups of flour;
  • 2 tablespoons of garlic salt;
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika;
  • 3 divided tablespoons of pepper;
  • 2½ of poultry seasoning;
  • 2 large eggs;
  • 1½ cups of water;
  • 1 teaspoon of salt;
  • 2 broiler/fryer chickens (3½ to 4 lbs each), cut up;
  • Oil for deep fat frying.

Steps

Preparing your chicken

  1. Making the flour mixture Combine 2⅔ cups of flour, garlic salt, paprika, 2½ teaspoons of pepper and poultry seasoning in a shallow dish.
  2. Making the egg mixture In another shallow dish, beat eggs and 1½ cups of water, add salt and the remaining 1⅓ cups of flour and ½ teaspoons of pepper.
  3. Coating the chicken Dip chicken in the egg mixture, and then dip the chicken in the flour mixture, a few pieces at a time. Remember to turn to coat.

Frying the chicken

  1. Heat oil to 375°C in your deep fat fryer;
  2. Put the chicken several pieces at a time into fryer once the fryer is heated to 375°C;
  3. Fry chicken pieces 7-8 minutes on each side until golden brown and juices run clear;
  4. Finally, drain on paper towels.

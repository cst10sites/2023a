Introduction:

This recipe is for macaroni and cheese. I very much like the taste of this mac and cheese and it's not too complicated.

Ingredients:

  • 10 oz macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1½ tablespoons flour
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1½ cups grated cheddar cheese

Instructions:

  1. Boil a large saucepan of water and add the macaroni. Cook for 5-7 minutes and then drain the water.
  2. While the water is boiling and the macaroni is cooking, place a frying pan on medium-low heat and add the butter.
  3. Once the butter is melted, add the flour and stir continuously with a whisk until the butter and flour becomes a thick paste.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the milk. Stir continuously until the mixture thickens (about 5 minutes).
  5. Add the grated cheddar and stir with a wooden spoon until the cheese melts, then turn off the heat.
  6. Once the macaroni is done, add it to the pan with the mixture. Stir it all together.

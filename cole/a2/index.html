My Recipe


ingredients:


  • 1 cup cream

  • 1 cup sugar

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter

  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda

  • 3/4 cups of powdered sugar

  • 1/4 cups of milk

  • 3/4 cups of butter

  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt


recipie


  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F.

  2. Grease a square baking pan and set aside for later.

  3. Combine 1 cup cream, 1 cup sugar, and ½ a cup of unsalted butter together in a mixing bowl.

  4. Add 2 eggs, one at a time, beating briefly after each addition.

  5. Mix in vanilla.

  6. Combine 1 ½ cups of all purpose flour and 1 ¾ cups of baking powder in a separate bowl.

  7. Add your wet ingredients into your dry ingredient bowl and mix well.

  8. Add ½ a cup of milk to your batter and stir until smooth.

  9. Pour your batter out into the previously set aside baking pan .

  10. Bake in the preheated oven until your finger no longer leaves indentation on the cakes surface (30 to 40 minutes.)

  11. Add ½ a cup of milk to your batter and stir until smooth.

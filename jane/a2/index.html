My Recipe: Pillsbury Pumpkin Shaped Sugar Cookies

Introduction

During the Halloween season, these cookies are one of the few things I look forward to. They are easy to make and only take 6 mintues to bake.

What you will need:

  • 1 box of Pillsbury Pumpkin Shaped Sugar Cookies
  • ½ cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of Butter
  • Sprinkles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 350°C (or 325°F for nonstick cookie sheet).
  2. Grease cookie sheet with butter.
  3. PLACE cookie dough rounds about 2 inches apart on greased cookie sheet.
  4. BAKE 10 to 14 minutes or until light golden brown. COOL 2 minutes; remove from cookie sheet.
  5. Pour milk into glass.
  6. (Optional) Decorate your cookies with sprinkles.
  7. ENJOY! ☺♥

Tip: If baking frozen dough, add 2 minutes to the bake time.

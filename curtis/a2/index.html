My Recipe: Grilled Cheese

Introduction

I chose grilled cheese because it is simple to make, and has a great taste and texture. The origins of grilled cheese date back to the 1920's, and is a staple of Western culture. Many people eat grilled cheese today and primarily eat it for lunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1½ tbsp of butter
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Spread your butter on both your slices of bread.
  2. Flip both slices over and put cheese on each slice.
  3. Set your oven to 350°F and bake for 3 minutes or if you have a toaster oven, set the heat to 3 and the time to 2.
  4. Combine both slices and serve.

Meditation: The Fix to All Your Problems

How To Start Meditating

There are various ways to meditate but the most simple version is just focusing on staying in the moment. Whether that be focusing on a particular sound or sensation. The point is to avoid wandering thoughts, but not to get annoyed when your mind wanders eventually. Most people like sitting in a quiet place with eyes closed while focusing on their breath. When you notice yourself focused on a thought, just bring your attention back to the current moment. You can start with a short session, like 2 or 3 minutes, then slowly work up to a longer session for more benefits.

Online Resources

There are so many different resources online where you can find directions for meditation. The one I would recommend starting with is Medito. Medito is a free, volunteer run mobile app but can also can be accessed on the web. You just click on a session with the time it will take displayed, and you follow the directions that the narrator you choose gives you. You may want to go through their lessons before doing the regular sessions. There are different sessions for different uses, such as getting ready for sleep or just calming down racing thoughts. You can even download some of these sessions for offline use.

Meditation: The Fix to All Your Problems

What is Meditation?

Meditation is becoming more aware of the things that happen in your life. It's about being able to make good decisions that are often hard to make, being more focused during your day, and being able to observe your thoughts without judgement. Some archaeologists believe that meditation started being practiced around 5000 BCE.

History of Meditation

Some archaeologists believe that meditation dates back to 5000 BCE. However the oldest found text mentioning meditation is from India and around 1500 BCE. These texts mentioned meditation being used in Hinduism1. Meditation was also referenced in China around 300 BCE, linked to Daoism2. In around the 1900's meditation began gaining popularity in the West from meditation masters and studies showing its benefits. Now, there are many apps and websites you can use to help guide your meditation.

  1. Hinduism is believed to be the world's oldest religion, dating back to around 4000 years ago. It is an Indian religion and some of its main beliefs include havbing a good life and acting morally.
  1. Daoism or Taoism is a mix between a religion and a philosophy that dates back to around 2600 years ago. Founded by Lao Tzu in ancient China, its main belief is that nature controls the things around us and not people.

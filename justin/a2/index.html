My recipe

Introduction

Today I'll be teaching you how to make salmon sashimi. I like this food because it has a good amount of fat and protien and can taste good.

Ingredients

You will need:

  • A whole sashimi grade salmon
  • A tube of wasabi
  • A bottle of soy sauce

Intructions

  1. Prepare a cutting board and a sharp knife.
  2. Clean and descale the salmon the best you can.
  3. Place salmon onto cutting board.
  4. Fillet the salmon with the knife and remove any bones with the knife or tweezer's.
  5. Cut the bone free fillet's into ½, ¼ segments using a clean knife.
  6. Cut the ½ or ¼ segements into slices of personal choice.
  7. After cutting place the meat onto a plate.
  8. Grab a small bowl and add the desirable amount of soy sauce and wasabi.
  9. Enjoy your homemade sashimi!

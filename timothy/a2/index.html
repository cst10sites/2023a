My Recipe:

Introduction:

I am going to be making chocolate chip cookies. These are my favourite because I used to bake them with my sister and they are pretty simple to make and delicious to eat.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup Becel original margarine
  • 1 ¼ cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • ½ tsp of salt
  • ½ cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • ¼cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • ¼tsp flaked sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Combine flour, baking soda and salt; set aside.
  2. In large bowl, beat together Becel Original, brown sugar and granulated sugar.
  3. Beat in egg and vanilla until blended. Gradually add flour mixture and beat just until blended. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Drop heaping tablespoonfuls of batter onto ungreased baking sheets, about 2 inches (5 cm) apart. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until edges are golden.
  5. Remove from oven; sprinkle with flaked sea salt. Let cool for 2 minutes in pan on rack; transfer cookies directly to rack and let cool completely.

Pacific staghorn sculpin

Description:Staghorn sculpins are slender fish, with a grayish olive above, pale creamy yellow sides, and a white belly. The first dorsal fin has 7 spines and usually a dark spot in the posterior half

Where to fish: Pacific staghorn sculpins are often found in shallow water near rocks and cover. larger sculpin sinhabit deeper waters while juvenilles are plentiful along the shoreline.

Season: Pacific staghorn sculpins can be caught year round

Baits and lures: Pacific staghorn sculpins while attack anything that is infront of them which makes them an ideal fishery for entry level anglers. baits to use are white curly tail grubs fish chunkjs shrimp and worms.

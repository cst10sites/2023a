Coastal cutthroat trout

Description: In the Tidal fraser river Coastal cutthroat trout are a very chrome silver an dlose many of their freshwater characteristics of heavy spotting. Coastal cutthroat trout in the fraser river vary from 1-4 pounds.

Where to fish: Look for structure when fishing for cutthroats. wood pilings overhanging trees often hold fish. also casting along the shoreline works good to.

Season:April-may august-october

Baits/lures: When using baits worms, singular eggs, and roe are often productive. spoons and spinners in pink, gold, and silver produce nicelly. you may also use artificial flies and jigs.

Ingredients 😊😊😊

  • Egg (depends on how many eggs you want to make)
  • Pan (to cook the egg on)
  • Oil or butter (to make pan slippery)
  • Spatula (to flip egg with)

How to make an egg!!!!🥚🍳

  1. Heat up your pan on the stove,
  2. Put some Oil or butter onto your pan and let it spread.
  3. Then crack the egg or mulitpile eggs onto the pan
  4. Wait for the albumen (aka the clear parts of the egg) to turn white and start to sizzle
  5. Gently slide your spatula underneath the egg, CAREFUL! not to jab too hard because otherwisethe egg will break
  6. Flip egg over, and wait 30 seconds until it stops sizzling
  7. Slide your spatula underneath and put on a plate and enjoy! 😊😊😊😊😊😊😊😊

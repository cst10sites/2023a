My Recipe

Introduction

This recipe is for the best lemonade in the world. This lemonade is the only liquid that I have drank in 20 years and it makes me feel like I am the strongest person ever. This recipe has been in my family for generations. For 200 years it has been kept a secret and today I am going share it with you.

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 9 medium lemons
  • 7 cups of water at 15°C
  • 5½ cups ice (Note: ice must have a temperature colder then 0°C)

Instructions

  1. Combine sugar and 1 cup water in a small saucepan. Stir to dissolve sugar while mixture comes to a boil. Set aside to cool slightly.
  2. Meanwhile, roll lemons around on your counter to soften. Cut in half lengthwise, and squeez into a liquid measuring cup. Add pulp to the juice, but discard any seeds. Continue juicing until you have 1½ cups of fresh juice and pulp.
  3. Pour 7 cups of ice-cold water into a pitcher. Stir in lemon juice and pulp, then add the simple syrup to taste. Add ice.

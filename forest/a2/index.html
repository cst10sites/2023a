Almond Tofu

✧Refreshing dishes✧

The meal

Today we're going to learn how to make Almond Tofu!, a traditional chinese summer desert that will refresh you in seconds!

I personally love this desert because it was introduced to me by my favourite game, and my favourite character. Also, it was summer a few weeks ago so it was the perfect time to eat a delicious desert like so.

✧Ingredients✧

  • 1½ cups of whole milk
  • 1½ cup of heavy milk
  • ½ cup of sugar
  • 1tbsp of almond extract
  • 1tsbp of gelatin
  • ½ cup of cold water
  • Slices of fruits of your choice!

✧Steps✧

  1. Bloom gelatin by mixing gelatin with ½ cup cold water
  2. Heat 1½ cups of milk and stir in sugar until dissolved and remove from stove
  3. Mix in the bloomed gelatin and make sure all gelatin has dissolved
  4. Add in ½ cup of heavy cream
  5. Pour into ramekins and let it cool to room temperature
  6. Refrigerate at least 6 hours for it to set
  7. Serve as is or top with sliced fruitm of your choice
  8. Enjoy!

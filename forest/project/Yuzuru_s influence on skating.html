Yuzuru Hanyu


Athletically

Yuzuru Hanyu has always been known as an icon, that one figure skater that some people know because they tuned into the Olympics once or twice. However he is much more than an icon, he performed very difficult programs and often injured himself because of them. He is the first one to reach the 100-point and 300-point barrier in figure skating, and the first to land a very difficult jump the "Quad Loop"

Influence on public

Before Yuzuru Hanyu became famous, people rarely watched men's figure skating, because it was usually blander and not interesting. However, when Yuzuru Hanyu entered the field, he himself changed the way people looked at men's figure skating.

Yuzuru Hanyu


Bio

Yuzuru Hanyu is a retired figure skater who was born on the 7th of December 1994. He is a 2-time Olympic champion (2014, 2018), and a 2-time world champion (2014, 2017). He is the first Asian man to win gold in the singles discipline and the first in 66 years to win 2 Olympic gold medals back to back. He is a survivor of the 2011 japan earthquake and struggles with asthma. However now he is one of Japan's top celebrities, and he is the most famous figure skater in the world.

Hope and Legacy

Parisienne walkways is a program he used in the 2012-2013 season and 2013-2014 seasons, with this program he won the 2014 Olympics and broke 4 world records.

Seimei is a program he used in the 2015-2016, 2017-2018, and 2019-2020 seasons, with this program he won the 2018 Olympics and broke 4 world records again.

Hope and Legacy is a program he used in the 2016-2017 season (my favourite), with this program he won his second world title, even with an asthma attack 2 weeks prior.

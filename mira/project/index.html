The Toronto Maple Leafs were founded on November 22 1917, making them one of six "original" NHL teams (along with: The Montreal Canadiens, Chicago Blackhawks, Detroit Red Wings, New York Rangers and Boston Bruins). The Toronto Maple Leafs are a professional Canadian men's hockey team based in Toronto, Ontario, they compete in the NHL (National Hockey League),

they are (as of november 2022) valued at 2.12 billion US dollars, making them the most valuable NHL franchise. Over the 105 years they have been a team the toronto maple leafs have won 13 stanley cups and have produced 71 Hall of Famers along the way.

Mitch Marner

Mitchel Marner was born in Markham Ontario, when he was young; he attended hockey academies and at the age of 19 (2016) he joined the Maple Leafs as a winger. Six years later (2022) he is still with them as an assistant captain and one of the most influential players on the leafs bench.

At the beginning of the 2022-2023 season Mitch decided he wanted something big this year, with only 29 games played so far, Marner has accumulated a total of 32 points with a streak of 23 games, putting him in the books. He is now the holder of the longest point streak in leafs history and 20th in the NHL's history. Marner was originally drafted by the Maple Leafs in the first round (4th overall) of the 2015 NHL Draft.

Auston Matthews

Auston Matthews is an American centre and alternate captain for the Toronto Maple Leafs. He was born in California but moved to Arizona as a baby. Before being signed to The Toronto Maple Leafs, he decided to play with the ZSC lions, a swiss team that plays in the swiss national league A. He was the number one overall draft pick in the 2016 NHL draft. In his debut game with the leafs, he scored 4 goals against the Detroit Red Wings; this was the first time in modern NHL history for a player to score 4 goals in their debut game. In the 2017-18 season he set the unofficial NHL record for consecutive games with a shot on goal; he had made it to 103 games until having to sit out for an injury. Season after season, Matthews continues to impress. In 2020-2021 season he recorded 41 goals in 56 games with a season total of 66 points. That year he became the first Leaf to win the Maurice “Rocket” Richard Trophy, awarded to the player with the most goals in regular season, also being the first american player to win. He was a Finalist for the Hart Trophy, being the first Leaf finalist since Doug Gilmour in 1993, he placed second only to Conner McDavid.

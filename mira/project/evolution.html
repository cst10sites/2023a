Evolution of What we Know to be The Toronto Maple Leafs

The Cup Evolution Famous Players Quiz

In 1906 the Toronto Professional Hockey Club was founded by Bruce Ridpath, a former player for the Toronto Malbaros who was banned by the OHA (Ontario hockey Association) with three other players for playing for money. He formed The Toronto Pros just days later, they played in the OPHL (Ontario Professional Hockey League) for 3 years before changing their name to The Toronto Blueshirts and switching to the NHA (National Hockey Association of Canada Limited) in 1912 where they stayed for 2 seasons until changing their name again and joining the NHL as the Toronto Arenas, that name only lasted 2 years but one them one stanley cup. In 1919 the Toronto Saint Pats were born, who are arguably the most memorable team, they lasted for 8 seasons until they were bought by Conn Smyth, who saved them from moving to philadelphia, he immediately rebranded their name to The Toronto Maple Leafs and their team colours to white and blue.

The Toronto Maple Leafs first joined the NHL in 1917, as the Toronto St Patricks, for those next 8 seasons they kept that name until finally changing it to the famous Toronto Maple Leafs in 1927. The Maple Leafs experienced major success during the “original six” era, dominating the league and winning 9 Stanley cups of the 25 awarded.

Even now in 2023 the Toronto Saint Pats jerseys are honoured by being worn every saint patrick's day.

Tag Definition Examples
<!DOCTYPE html> Placed at the very top of your page to indicate that the document is html.
<html lang="en"> used to indicate what language your document is in, for translation purpises (en means englih and can be chnage to any other language).
title The title is showcased on the tab.
charset="UTF-8" charset is an atribute that goes inside the meta tag, it specifies the character encoding for html document.
<style>...</style> Used for internal CSS: to make CSS in the same HTML document as your other code.
favicon Image beside the title on your website, could be your logog or brand name.
<link rel="stylesheet" href="styles.css"> Used for external CSS: once you create a style.css document you can be more organized by working on your CSS separately.

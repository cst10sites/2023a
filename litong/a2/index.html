The food I'm going to make is scrambled eggs, I like to make scrambled eggs beacause its very easy to make, fast to make, and tasty.

Ingredients

  1. 3 Eggs
  2. 1 green onion
  3. a spoon of minced garlic
  4. ½Spoon of vinegar
  5. ¼Cup oil

Instrustions

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl
  2. Cut the green onion and put both of green onion and minced garlic in to egg liquid
  3. Put vinegar in to egg liquid and stir well
  4. Put some oil on the pan, heat up, and put egg liquid in to the pan
  5. start frying

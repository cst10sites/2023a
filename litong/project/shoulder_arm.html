Shoulder & arm & Abs






V Push ups

  1. Get down on the floor, start with pushup position.
  2. Bend your butt to 130°.
  3. Use your shoulder muscle get down and up.
  4. Try to hold your core muscle and put pressure on your shoulder

Chin Ups

  1. Hold your hand on the bar.
  2. Use your biceps to pull you up over your chin.

Dips

  1. Get into position with a bench behind you.
  2. Tighten your core and lower your body off the bench.
  3. Keep your head and upper body straig.
  4. Move yourself up and down without returning to the bench.

Plank

  1. Get down on the floor, hands flat on the floor.
  2. Tight your core muscle
  3. Keeping your forearms and knees on the floor slowly raise yourself upwards until your body is in a straight line from your knees to your head.
  4. Hold the position for as long as you can.

Bar Knee Raise

  1. Start by hanging on a pull-up bar.
  2. Use your core muscle as you raise knees toward chest
  3. Lean forward,put pressure on to chest muscle.
  4. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor.
  5. Pause, then push yourself back up.
